About Us

  • Educational Startup founded in 2015
  • Courses on Robotics for school kids
  • Successfully conducted various workshops on Robotics, Python and latest technologies
  • Our aim is to provide online courses on Robotics and other technologies to school & college students
  • Launched CodeSchool Series for School & College students in 2016
{% comment %}

PNT Academy is an educational startup that was founded in 2015. We provide courses on robotics to school kids. We have successfully conducted various workshops on robotics, Python, and latest technologies.

The company is a result of the passion for knowledge and learning. It was started in 2015 with an aim to provide online courses on Robotics and other technologies to school & college students .

In 2016, we launched CodeSchool Series for School and College students. This series is designed to enhance the knowledge of latest technologies related to robotics to School kids and provide free mentorships on placement guidance to college students. We are proud to announce our recent launch of CodeSchool Series 2, which has been designed specifically for college students and provides them with real-world experience through practical assignments that includes coding challenges and projects.
